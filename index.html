Transportation

Travel by air or cruise ship, then get around by bus, taxi, rental car, or bike.

Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, with euros and yen accepted in many places.

Safety

Violent crime is rare, though travelers should watch for petty theft.

Travel Tips

Some businesses close on holidays, and English is less common in rural areas.
