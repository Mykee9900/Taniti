Travel Information

Transportation

Visitors arrive by air or cruise ship and can get around Taniti by bus, taxi, rental car, or bike.

Map & Getting Around

Taniti City is walkable, while Merriton Landing is easy to explore on foot. Public and private buses serve other areas of the island.

Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, though many businesses also accept euros and yen. Major credit cards are accepted in many places.

Safety

Violent crime is rare, but visitors should stay aware of petty theft and plan around holiday closures at attractions and restaurants.
